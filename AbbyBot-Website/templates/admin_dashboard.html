Welcome to the AbbyBot Admin Panel. Use the buttons below to manage the bot's data and settings.

Manage Messages Manage Wishlists Manage Contributors

Instructions

  • To manage bot messages, click the "Manage Messages" button. This section allows you to view the messages received by users and sent by AbbyBot.
  • To manage wish lists, use the "Manage Wish Lists" button. You can list all users along with their forms in the bot's wishlist feature.
  • To manage contributors, go to the "Manage contributors" section. This allows you to track and adjust the members of the hall-of-fame page, along with viewing, adding/removing contributions made by a user.
